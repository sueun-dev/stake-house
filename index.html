Simple Staking Framework

1

Mint or Purchase NFTs

Acquire our StakeHouse NFTs through ETH or supported ERC20 tokens. Multiple minting options available including whitelist access and public sale.

2

Deposit NFTs for Staking

Connect your wallet and deposit your NFTs into our secure staking contract. Multiple NFTs can be staked in a single transaction for greater efficiency.

3

Earn Time-Based Rewards

Our smart contract automatically tracks staking duration and calculates daily rewards based on our transparent reward formula.

4

Claim $SH Rewards

Claim your accumulated $SH tokens at any time with no minimum staking period required. Continue staking your NFTs for uninterrupted rewards.

5

Exchange or Use $SH Tokens

Use your earned $SH tokens within our ecosystem or trade them on supported exchanges for other cryptocurrencies.
